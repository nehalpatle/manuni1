Manuni Inn – Luxury Heritage Hotel Gallery

Hotel (Exterior & Interior)

Manuni Inn, a luxurious heritage hotel at Fort Kochi - one of the oldest cosmopolitan port cities in the world.

Luxury Rooms (Classic, Imperial & Sovereign Rooms)

Each of the 27 heritage abodes at Manuni Inn bespeaks of the glorious riches of colonial India.

Restaurant & Dining - Jetty

Offering choice delicacies from across the world on a platter, we invite you to anchor at the Jetty and relish varied cuisines that reflect the various cultures that once influenced Fort Kochi including authentic local Kerala flavours, pan Indian, pan European, British favorites, a special range of seafood choices and so much more.

Swimming Pool

Set in the central courtyard of the hotel, with a natural well on a side, the swimming pool beckons for a lazy swim, a refreshing lap or just a nice snooze under the sun.
