Manuni Inn Heritage Hotel welcomes you

Camera cannot depict the actual sensation one can get at the real site you got to visit to see it for real….

At an altitude of 1315 M above sea level at Dharamshala, Manuni Inn offers a sublime slice of elegant 21 rooms on the Himalayan foothills, with an emphasis on elegant simplicity. Manuni Inn is located right in the lap of the picturesque steep Dhauladhar Mountains, in the enchanting village of Khanyara, Dharamshala.

The accommodation at the Hotel Manuni Inn is perfect for everyone wanting to enjoy nature in peace and comfort in their own leisure. The hotel rooms are built in style with all Western comforts, reminding you of casual and roomy ambience. Our guests are always surrounded by the beauty of the region as panoramic windows have been installed in all our rooms for spectacular natural views.

Come, step into yesteryear, into a world gracefully tucked in the glories of the past, brimming with regal heritage and plush luxury, and as far away from the present as possible!

Manuni Inn shines like a pearl among the hotels in Manuni Inn - one of the oldest cosmopolitan port cities in the world.

Its tree hemmed streets, sun-kissed shores and tall ancient edifices still bear remnants of past eras.

And in one such imposing yet unassuming structure is Manuni Inn. Originally built by the Portuguese, the mansion presents a medley of Portuguese, Dutch and British influences and was converted to a magnificent home in the late 1800s. This ancient structure was once part of the palatial home of an eminent Jew family.

Now restored to its past glory, this boutique hotel enchants its visitors with glimpses of life once known and lived, centuries ago.

Step into one of the best Heritage Hotels in India and step into an age so far away.

Location

A Boutique Hotel in the midst of Dharamshala Cultural District

Manuni Inn is located on the beautiful Princess Street - The Crowning Jewel of Manuni Inn.

Just a leisurely 30-minute drive from Ernakulam (Cochin) Junction (the nearest train station) and an hour's drive from Cochin International Airport, Hotel Manuni Inn is a short distance from all of Manuni Inn's innumerable attractions.

Start your day with a walk to Vasco De Gama square and stroll along the picturesque Beach Walkway on Mahatma Gandhi Beach or marvel at the nearby Chinese fishing nets - a centuries-old remnant of the trade route, and now an indelible part of Kochi's heritage.

A memorable ferry ride lets you immerse yourself in the history of India at the culturally significant Our Lady of Hope Church and a short drive will take you to the famous Mattanchery Palace.

The tree-lined boulevards around Manuni Inn offer myriads of cuisines, that is, if you're inclined to step out from our in-house multi-cuisine restaurant - The Jetty! True to the city's multi-cultural roots, Jetty offers a choice of British, pan-European, and Indian dishes. Don't miss out on the local Kerala cuisine and seafood dishes on offer!

Colonial-Style Rooms

Twenty-seven carefully crafted luxury rooms await you at this heritage hotel.

Each room is a gorgeous mix of old world charm and modern luxury amenities. Colourfully tinted windows, solid wooden furniture, and large, inviting four poster beds welcome you at first sight.

On second glance, the cushioned wicker sofas, a medley of coloured tiles interspersed with wooden floors and the vivid dish of Portuguese summer colours take your breath away and transport you to colonial India.
